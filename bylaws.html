H and J By Laws

Article I: Name and Purpose

The official business name is H and J.

The company specializes in jewelry repair, maintenance, customization, and secure jewelry delivery services.

Article II: Business Structure and Management

The company is a sole proprietorship/partnership (modify as needed).

The business is managed by the owner(s), with employees handling repairs, customer service, and deliveries.

Decision-making responsibilities include service pricing, logistics, customer relations, and business growth strategies.

Article III: Jewelry Services

The business provides services such as resizing, polishing, stone setting, and general repairs.

Customization and restoration services are available based on customer preferences.

All services follow a pricing structure based on material costs and labor.

Article IV: Store, Online, and Delivery Operations

The physical store shall operate within designated business hours.

Online orders, inquiries, and bookings shall be managed by dedicated personnel.

Jewelry delivery services shall be available for both online and in-store orders.

Secure packaging and delivery tracking shall be implemented to ensure customer satisfaction and safety.

Policies for service turnaround times, deposits, warranties, and deliveries shall be clearly communicated.

Article V: Financial and Legal Policies

All transactions, including repairs, sales, and deliveries, must be documented for financial tracking.

Compliance with tax regulations, business permits, and other legal requirements is mandatory.

Article VI: Employee and Customer Code of Conduct

Employees must uphold honesty, professionalism, and customer service excellence.

Customers are expected to respect business policies regarding payments, warranties, deliveries, and repairs.

Article VII: Amendments and Modifications

The by-laws may be reviewed and amended as necessary for the growth and stability of the business.
